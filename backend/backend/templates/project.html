What is our Concept?

There is a vast amount of data in the world of the internet. We may want to just copy-paste some tabular data from a website, yet it is hard to keep the structure and format of the cells and data when the table is copy-pasted as text. We are aiming to efficiently extract these tables in a standard, editable DataFrame format. They provide a clear and concise way to display data, making it easier for readers to understand and interpret the information. For this purpose, we have focused on developing a web browser extension called ''Table Catcher''.
